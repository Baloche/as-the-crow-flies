A minimalist navigation app for self-guiding humans.

As the crow flies is a minimalist navigation app originally designed for adventurous cyclists wanting to keep an unobtrusive eye on their destination, but it's also well suited for pedestrians wanting to get through their city without having a route imposed on them.
